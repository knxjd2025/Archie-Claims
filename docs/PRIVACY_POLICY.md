# Privacy Policy — Archie Claims

_Last updated: June 9, 2026_

Archie Claims ("the app") is built so that your data stays on your device. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with information.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** The app has no accounts, no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no server operated by us. We (the developer) do not receive, store, or sell any data about you or the homes you canvass.

## What stays on your device

- **Leads** (addresses, homeowner names, phone numbers, notes, storm snapshots) are stored only in the app's local storage on your iPhone and in your device backups. Deleting the app deletes them.
- **Your Anthropic API key** is stored in the iOS Keychain on your device.
- **Settings** (search radius, lookback window) are stored in local app preferences.
- **Your location** is used only to center the canvassing map while the app is open. It is never transmitted to us or stored.

## What leaves your device

- **Storm data requests** — when you tap a house, the app requests public storm data from NOAA/NWS government servers (`spc.noaa.gov`, `api.weather.gov`). These requests include the coordinates being looked up and are subject to those services' policies.
- **AI assistant messages** — if you add your own Anthropic API key and chat with Archie, your messages (and any property storm context you attach) are sent directly from your device to Anthropic (`api.anthropic.com`) to generate responses, under your own Anthropic account and [Anthropic's privacy policy](https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy). Don't include information in chats that you don't want processed by the AI provider.
- **Public records links** — lookup buttons open third-party public websites (e.g. county assessor directories, people-search sites) in an in-app browser. Those sites have their own privacy policies; the app does not read, scrape, or store anything from them.

## Your responsibilities

Information about homeowners that you enter or look up must be used in compliance with applicable laws — including telemarketing rules (TCPA / do-not-call), local solicitation ordinances, and do-not-knock lists.

## Children

The app is a business tool intended for users 18+. It does not target or knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes

If a future version of the app changes any of the above (for example, adds optional cloud sync), this policy and the App Store privacy label will be updated first.
